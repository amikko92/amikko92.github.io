About

My name is Andreas Mikko. I grew up in the small town of Kiruna in northern Sweden. But these days I live in Trollhättan together with my wife and daughter.

My development journey started in my final year of high school. Were I made a simple space shooter game with Game Maker. This first project motivated me to pursue a University degree in game development. Game design was my initial plan but I eventually realized I had a greater talent for programming rather than design.

Currently, I work as a Lead Engineer at Evolution in Gothenburg, Sweden. Where I make slot games under the Netent brand. Previous merits include lecturing at Södertörn University and Microsoft Azure development at Semcon.
